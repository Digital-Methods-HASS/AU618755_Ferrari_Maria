Translation of metadata from Danish to English for citizenship records 1740 1862 provided by Aarhus Stadsarkiv https://github.com/aarhusstadsarkiv/datasets/tree/master/citizenship/1740-1862:

(from right to left)

Arkiv: archive
Bye: city
Herred: district
Amt: county
Sognenr: parish number
Recnr: record number
Folie: folder
Aar: year
Borgerskabsdag: day of citizenship
Bordered: citizenship-oath
Borgerskabsdato: date of citizenship
Fornavn: first name
Efternavn: last name
Oprindelsessted: place of origin
Land: country
Alder: age
Hovederhverv: main profession
Noter: notes

-

Explanation of metadata:

years_liv: amount of years lived
Avg_years_liv: average amount of years lived
